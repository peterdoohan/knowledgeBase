## Key evidence

- Psychotic symptoms in schizophrenia—including positive symptoms and auditory perceptual abnormalities—correrelate with cortical disinhibition (decreased self-inhibition), opposing the primary group-level hyperinhibition pattern. ([Adams 2021](../../raw/summaries/adams2021_pyramidal_synaptic_gain.md))
- Compensatory interneuron downregulation (disinhibition) may destabilise cortical circuit dynamics, creating a narrow therapeutic window for glutamatergic treatments that restore pyramidal gain without addressing the compensatory rebalancing. ([Adams 2021](../../raw/summaries/adams2021_pyramidal_synaptic_gain.md))
