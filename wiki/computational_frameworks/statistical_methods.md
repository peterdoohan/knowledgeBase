## Key evidence

- P-value threshold for claiming new discoveries should be lowered from 0.05 to 0.005 ([Benjamin2018](../../raw/summaries/benjamin2018_statistical_significance.md))

- Empirical replication rates approximately double when initial studies used P < 0.005 versus 0.005 < P < 0.05 ([Benjamin2018](../../raw/summaries/benjamin2018_statistical_significance.md))

- Maintaining 80% power under the new threshold requires approximately 70% larger sample sizes ([Benjamin2018](../../raw/summaries/benjamin2018_statistical_significance.md))
