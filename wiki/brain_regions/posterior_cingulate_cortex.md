## Key evidence

- Posterior cingulate cortex shows exploratory effects of prediction error by structure interaction, suggesting potential involvement in structure-sensitive learning signals. Exploratory whole-brain analysis revealed prediction error × structure interaction effects in posterior cingulate cortex alongside vmPFC and ventral striatum; this region is also known for grid-like coding and schema representations in other work ([Baram 2021](../../raw/summaries/baram2021_entorhinal_ventromedial_rl.md))
