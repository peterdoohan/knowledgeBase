# Anterior Prefrontal Cortex (antPFC)

The anterior prefrontal cortex (antPFC), also known as frontopolar cortex or BA 10, is the most anterior portion of the prefrontal cortex in primates. It is implicated in high-level cognitive functions including hierarchical planning, relational reasoning, and prospective memory.

## Key evidence

- Anterior PFC encodes the longest predictive horizons (up to ~875 m) while posterior hippocampus encodes the shortest (~25 m), demonstrating a multiscale successor-representation hierarchy across hippocampal and prefrontal gradients; fMRI RSA during naturalistic virtual navigation with gamma values 0.1-0.9 corresponding to 25-875m horizons; antPFC showed significant above-zero similarity for all tested horizons including 10 TRs ahead (~250 m) ([Brunec 2022](../../raw/summaries/brunec2022_predictive_representations_hierarchies.md))

- Goal-directed navigation shows longer and stronger predictive representations than GPS-guided navigation in antPFC, with top-down goal representations shaping spatial scale of predictive coding; gamma x condition interaction F(2,1448)=7.49, p<0.001; goal-directed condition showed enhanced antPFC and aHPC predictive similarity at longer horizons; antPFC > mPFC > aHPC > pHPC in magnitude of predictive similarity (F(3,1448)=547.38, p<0.001, eta_p^2=0.53) ([Brunec 2022](../../raw/summaries/brunec2022_predictive_representations_hierarchies.md))

- Path distance modulates predictive representations in antPFC: longer routes are associated with greater representational similarity; mixed-effects model F(1,149)=91.51, p<0.001, eta_p^2=0.38; this contrasts with hippocampus where shorter routes showed higher representational similarity, suggesting as navigation scale increases, PFC increasingly carries global planning representation while hippocampal representations remain locally scoped ([Brunec 2022](../../raw/summaries/brunec2022_predictive_representations_hierarchies.md))

- Anterior/polar PFC (BA 10) shows the largest coverage of significantly predictive voxels across gamma values in whole-brain searchlight analysis; OFC (BA 11) and ACC (BA 25/32) follow; dorsolateral PFC (BA 9, 46) shows modest overlap; IFG (BA 45) shows none ([Brunec 2022](../../raw/summaries/brunec2022_predictive_representations_hierarchies.md))

- Trial-by-trial hippocampal goal evidence positively correlates with planning-period activity in lateral and medial frontopolar cortex, suggesting PFC-hippocampal coupling during prospective simulation; FPC activity tracked trial-by-trial goal coding strength in hippocampus ([Brown 2016](../../raw/summaries/brown2016_prospective_goals_hippocampus.md))
