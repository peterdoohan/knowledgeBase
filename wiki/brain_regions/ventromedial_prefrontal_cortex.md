## Key evidence

- vmPFC and ventral striatum encode prediction errors in structure-specific multivoxel patterns, suggesting structure-conditioned credit assignment. Multivoxel patterns of prediction error signals differed by relational structure in left vmPFC (p=0.014) and right vmPFC (p=0.02), with univariate PE signals being strong in vmPFC (t(27)≈9.3-9.4) ([Baram 2021](../../raw/summaries/baram2021_entorhinal_ventromedial_rl.md))

- vmPFC exhibits a longer hemodynamic response function for prediction error signals compared to ventral striatum, peaking at approximately 8.5 seconds versus 6 seconds post-outcome. This suggests region-specific hemodynamic dynamics or processing delays in vmPFC for PE signals ([Baram 2021](../../raw/summaries/baram2021_entorhinal_ventromedial_rl.md))
